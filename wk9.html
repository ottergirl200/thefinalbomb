CHINDOGU,
AND THE ACT OF CREATING MACHINES FOR THE SAKE OF MACHINES

Chindogu are machines that defy concise explanation; "un-useless" as their creator, Kenji Kawakami, describes.

They are invented objects that have no practical use, and are often humorous, unconventional, and satirical.

Shoe Umbrellas, Kenji Kawakami


Bruno Munari's book "Machines" (Le Macchine di Munari) also embodies this sense of machine creation for the sake of machines.

It details a collection of fantastical inventions not intended for function.

Le Macchine di Munari, Bruno Munari
